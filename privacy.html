Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Introduction

SoulGuide ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for spiritual guidance and meditation.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Spiritual Journey Data:

SoulGuide processes your spiritual journey information, meditation sessions, and soul insights locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your spiritual data to our servers or any third-party services. All processing and AI-powered guidance happens entirely on your device.

Meditation Records:

Your meditation session records, spiritual notes, and journey progress are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Usage Data:

We do not collect analytics data or usage statistics. SoulGuide operates entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage:

The app stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Meditation session records and progress
  • Spiritual journey notes and insights
  • Daily practice tracking and rituals
  • App settings and preferences
  • Favorite meditations and content

How We Use Your Information

We use locally stored information to:

  • Provide personalized spiritual guidance and soul insights
  • Track your meditation practice and spiritual journey
  • Remember your preferences and favorite content
  • Provide daily practices and rituals
  • Organize and manage your spiritual library
  • Remember your app settings and preferences

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your spiritual journey data and meditation records remain on your device.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

Your spiritual journey data and meditation records remain on your device. We do not transmit or store any spiritual data externally. While we take steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Your spiritual journey data and app preferences are stored locally and remain there until you delete the app or reset your settings. We do not retain any personal data or spiritual information on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

SoulGuide does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting services, or cloud storage services. All processing is done locally on your device.

AI Spiritual Guidance Technology

Our AI-powered spiritual guidance technology uses on-device machine learning models to provide soul insights and personalized guidance. No spiritual data or meditation records are sent to external servers for processing.

App Store Compliance

SoulGuide complies with both Apple's App Store and Google Play Store guidelines and privacy requirements:

  • No tracking or analytics collection
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Local-only data processing
  • Transparent privacy practices
  • Cross-platform consistency

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about our data practices

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@soulguide.app
  • Website: https://soulguide.app
